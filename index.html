Find Out My Flood Risk

Use this interactive map to see how your suburb can be affected by floods of different likelihood levels in your area.

This map is best viewed with Chrome, Safari, Firefox or your mobile browser.

For information on local flooding or flash flooding, or for property-level flood information, please contact your local council directly.

Disclaimer: This is a demonstration prototype. The flood data shown is sourced from publicly available flood awareness datasets and is intended for general awareness purposes only. It does not constitute professional advice.
